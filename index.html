I. The idea
What is Elev8 with T?

You have been personally invited because you are exactly the kind of person this circle is built for. Financially curious, growth-minded, and someone whose company I genuinely value.

Elev8 with T is a monthly gathering at my place. No agenda beyond good food, good conversation, and a shared commitment to making sharper financial decisions together. Think of it as your most financially literate friends in a room, pooling ideas and capital.

Each session, we eat together, talk freely, then get into the numbers. We will each pitch our top investment ideas, vote on the best three, pool a combined fund, and track performance for the month. Same time, next month. We review, we learn, we go again.

✦
III. The collective fund
How the fund works

At each gathering, every member contributes an equal amount into a shared pool. Through discussion and a group vote, we select the top 3 financial decisions, whether that is stocks, indices, crypto, a business move, or any other instrument the group agrees on.

The combined fund is then allocated equally across those three picks. Over the following 30 days, we monitor performance together. At the next session, we review results openly, wins and losses alike, and decide how to proceed.

This is not just about returns. It is about developing the discipline, vocabulary, and instincts to make better financial decisions as individuals and as a circle.
